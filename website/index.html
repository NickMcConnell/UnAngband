Unangband: The Unnamed Angband

 

Welcome to Unangband, my variant of Angband. Angband has a long history of development: I recommend you check out the excellent resources of thangorodrim.angband.org and angband.oook.cz if you are interested in the history and current state of the play for this game.

 

This page is here primarily as a place-holder: I’m going to be in Europe on a working holiday until the end of 2003, so its best to contact me in the forums at this website for all Unangband related questions. Andrew Sidwell is acting as a caretaker during this period; he is developing his own Angband variant, Nangband, so his primary focus is elsewhere. Just remember that if you start bugging either of us for support, we probably don’t have the time or energy to help you at this stage.

 

Having said that, I hope I haven’t put you off. I highly recommend you download the latest version of Unangband and start playing. Don’t forget to upload your character dumps to the Angband Ladder at angband.oook.cz and get bragging...
